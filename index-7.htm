Skvllpvnkz: An interactive collection of 10,000 machine generated avatar NFTs

With the transformative power of blockchain and a shared vision for a decentralised future, a dedicated team of artists, coders and misfits initiated the Skvllpvnkz.

Sophisticated computer algorithms stored on the Ethereum blockchain were employed to create 10,000 unique characters, all with distinctive traits. More than 350 delicately compiled, hand-drawn layers were stitched together and fed through the machine. Boundaries between art and code were questioned, bent, then broken.

The collection features 6 rare designs by an inspired collaboration of artists, which have been minted and auctioned to raise funds for a range of charities. 4 unique 1/1 lore characters are yet to be found! THE STORY

Past, present, future...

Years ago living under the shadows, a group of shady individuals represented what we would call in our modern days as freedom of will. Created to preserve people's rights and to stop the leaders of various races from their dictatorial ways, the order of the Skvll was what set the foundations and beliefs of what the now called Skvllpvnkz represent. Their ancestors fought for freedom, they fight for decentralization. Years into the future, the 100 OG Skvllpvnkz have grown in numbers. Owning the streets of the block-chain, big regulatory corporations decided to take action and push them back. Using enforcing SkvllBots to fight the Skvllpvnkz, the bots forced the remaining of the Skvllpvnk resistance into underground bunkers called The Hideouts. Trying to survive they go out on regular scavenger hunts where they can find valuable loot and other collectibles.

THE PREVIOUS COLLECTIONS

Backed by a 1/1 art project, story and lore

Skvllpvnkz is an art concept initially created by Fors. The NFT collectible was first launched and co-founded with Fofos. The collection was created in early May and successfully managed to keep instant daily sales from day one across all projects. The 5 Legendary Skvllpvnkz pushed the audio-visual boundaries of collectible avatars with innovative presentations. After the completion of the first project and all 100/100 pieces sold, the new collection of Skvllpvnkz Originz was released as a prequel to the first collection. Originz dives in to the past of the Skvllpvnkz and explores all the different kinds of Skvllkind through a vast variety of sketch-like 2D art. It was created to be different than the first collection but still keep the same feel. Originz conists of 100 original pieces including various different races, which then can be combined to created fusions for the rest 50 pieces. SkvllGodz are ancient gods worshiped by Skvllpvnkz of the past and also the present. 101 Godz 3D gods created to push the boundaries of quality NFTs and bring a dark artistic touch to collectibles. They are made of 2 factions split between 50 Havoc and 50 Accord Godz as well as 1 Rogue.

How are the Skvllpvnkz generated?

Using our own very special home-grown algorithm we precisely stitch together a selection of hand drawn layers such as masks, helmets, accessories and clothing. The algorithm is such that some combinations of properties, often called traits, will be more rare than others.
