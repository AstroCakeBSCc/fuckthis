ABOUT THE PROJECT

The Ethereans is a fantastic universe built by 2 creative brothers, The Chaos Duo.
These 2  brothers are focused on creating experiences, narratives, characters, high-end graphics, and cool animations. In order to achieve this, we have embarked on a mission to fund and grow The Chaos Duo Studio and bring more people to Crypto and NFT's.

THE ETHEREANS STORYLINE:
This universe of super cool characters is about an alien race from Planet Crypto, located far far away in the ETH region of the Milky Way Galaxy.  The Etherean Emperor, in search of discovery, started to launch expeditions to every corner of the galaxy.  In their expeditions, these folks discovered a new element that they called Ether . This element is capable of giving superpowers to whoever uses it, but the users also pay a big price when they use Ether in excess. Ether is also capable of satisfying the hunger of a living thing for several days and it's used as fuel for traveling at lightspeed.

THE CHAOS DUO OBJECTIVE:

The project's main goal is to create a big bang brand that produces series, short films, merch, toys, video games for the metaverse and traditional platforms beginning with PC, and then consoles, mobile devices, etc. which generates utility for all the supporters (token holders).

DEPLOY AND INVASION

STAGE 01

Avatar Collection

The Etherean Empire invasion will be landing on Earth with an army of 11,000 silly yet cute ether-thirsty soldiers with a minting price of 0.08 ETH in our public sale day on September 3rd at 10:00 am PST.

We are joining forces with Ether Cards so the first 1000 people in our discord and 1000 people from the Ether Cards community will have access to a 10% off on the minting price and the pre-sale on September 1st at 10:00 am PST with a max of two Ethereans per wallet with a limit of 4,000 Ethereans to deploy. We will update this with more info soon.

They will be deployed by Ranks: Soldiers, Captains, and Generals and minted randomly using Chainlink VRF.

The Ethereans love technology, so they created Cyborgs to help them with their laboratory experiments, engage in battles, and even household chores (so cool, right?).

Beware, you may find some failed laboratory experiments around artificial intelligence called Mutants and one or two Aberrations that may not be so friendly.

1,000 Ethereans will be held on the project wallet. A small portion of those Ethereans will be used for Giveaways and Content Creation, the rest will be for the benefit of the community (See Phase 2, Stage 1, Tokenomics below).

Date / September 2021

STAGE 02

3D Prints & Art Toys.

We are 3d print enthusiasts and we are bullish about the future of 3D printing but we also know that there's a lot of frustration related to 3D prints taking several hours/days and failed prints ending in a lot of wasted plastic or resin. That being said we are working on a base model with super easy to print traits for our token holders, if you want to go further and edit the model feel free to do so and share it with the community. A dedicated channel to chat about 3D printing will be created.

We are in talks with well-established manufacturers to make a deal and bring toys of The Ethereans into the market, this is a business model we are looking to explore and the milestone to achieve is to share the profits of it with our token holders. This is being analyzed and we will announce everything in our discord soon.
