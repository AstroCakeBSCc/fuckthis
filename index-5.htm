We Are Koin Games

“A one of a kind game dev studio, building player owned games with our players”

Koin games is a leading game studio in the industry disrupting PLAY-2-EARN movement, with the vision to create original and extremely fun player-owned blockchain games.

Our goal is to build player governed cryptocurrency economies. To develop games that , provide our players the ability to OWN, SELL, and TRADE every single Asset they Earn or Unlock in our Games! Our in game assets will have verifiable scarcity, and integrated secondary market to make trading and selling extremely simple and easy!

Every member of the Koin Games family has a passion for gaming and blockchain, and we consider it a privilege to work closely with our players and community to build something special. We want to invite you to join the VIDEO GAME DEV SQUAD (VGDS)!
